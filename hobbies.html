My main interests are automobiles and computers; but I'm also deeply interested in anything to do with engineering or anything that helps structure our society or thought (like history and politics!)

Automobiles

Cars were my first and are still one of my largest interests. From a very young age, I was interested in sports cars, F1 cars, and anything that was fast; especially Team Lotus F1 cars (my favourites being the 25, 49, 56, 72, 79, and the 88; though not necessarily in that order).

As I got older, I started to get interested in all kinds of intelligently engineered speed machinery, but two manufacturers have always stood out to me: Lotus and Porsche. Porsche's ruthless technical domination of endurance racing (starting with my favourite endurance car, the 917), as well as their continual leadership in powertrain design (largely exemplified by their astounding Porsche-Cisitalia 360) has always enthralled me; while Lotus's ruthless commitment to lightness and technical innovation (best summed up in one of my favourite Lotus road cars, the Elite Type 14) has been a great source of inspiration to me.
